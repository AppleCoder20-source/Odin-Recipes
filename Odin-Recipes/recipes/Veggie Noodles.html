Noodles

Description:

This is a very popular meal, found in all across the world

Even anime such as Naruto has a lot of references to ramen noodles

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 green onions, sliced, divided
  • 2 carrots, cut into matchsticks
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 9 oz dried rice noodles, cooked

Steps:

  1. Heat vegetable oil and and garlic and 3 green onions
  2. Cook for 2-3 mins
  3. Add the noodles and toss for a couple of minutes, until the noodles are well-coated and the vegetables are tender.
  4. Enjoy
